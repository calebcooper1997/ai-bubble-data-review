## What AI Bubble Is

AI Bubble explores the economics behind the current artificial intelligence surge and asks whether the huge amount of capital moving into AI is backed by durable value or by speculation that is running ahead of fundamentals. Using structured analysis and investigative reporting, the project highlights how America's trillion-dollar push into AI may depend on assumptions that have not been tested closely enough.

The project is aimed at economists, investors, policy analysts, and anyone interested in where technology meets finance. AI Bubble presents evidence in a transparent way, supported by clear visualizations. Instead of taking market optimism at face value, it follows the data, the hype, and the possible gap between what is being promised and what is actually happening in the AI sector.
